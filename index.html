General FAQs

Recruitment & Staffing FAQs

  • What recruitment services does GlobalHRMC offer?

    We offer end-to-end recruitment solutions including Overseas Recruitment, Executive Search, Permanent & Temporary Staffing, Payroll Management, and Visa Assistance across the GCC.

  • Which regions in the GCC do you cover?

    Our target market spans the entire GCC region, with active placements and offices supporting clients in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Bahrain.

  • How does your visa and immigration support work?

    We handle the entire visa processing, mobilization, and compliance documentation, ensuring a smooth transit and legal onboarding for international candidates.

  • How quickly can you supply skilled workforce talent?

    With a database of over 50,000+ vetted professionals, we can present matching resumes within 48 to 72 hours, depending on the role specialization.
